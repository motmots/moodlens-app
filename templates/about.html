About MoodLens

MoodLens is an innovative Artificial Intelligence (AI)-based project designed to detect and recognize human facial emotions in real-time. The goal of this project is to help users better understand their own emotional spectrum and explore the psychological meaning behind each expression.

Behind the Technology

MoodLens is powered by a custom EfficientNet architecture. We specifically trained our model using the FER-2013 dataset to accurately classify 7 core emotions: Happy, Sad, Angry, Fear, Disgust, Surprise, and Neutral in real-time.
