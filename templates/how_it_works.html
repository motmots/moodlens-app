How Does It Work?

A simple guide to using MoodLens emotion detection technology.

1

Allow Camera Access

Open the Live Detection page on this website. Your browser will request permission to use your laptop or device's camera. Click "Allow" (Izinkan) so our system can start viewing your face.

2

Real-Time AI Analysis

Once the camera is on, point your face toward the screen. Automatically, our Artificial Intelligence (AI) system will capture snapshots of your face every few seconds and process them using a Deep Learning (DeepFace) model to guess your emotion.

3

View Your Emotion Results

Within seconds, the dominant emotion (such as Happy, Sad, Angry, etc.) will appear on the screen. Try changing your expression and see how our AI responds to these changes!

4

Learn About the Emotion

You can visit the Emotions Spectrum menu to learn more about each emotion, why humans experience them, and the conditions under which they arise.
